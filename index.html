Be Well. Be Kempt.

Kempt Soap Co. is a small soap company located in Louisville KY. We specialize in all natural, small-batch, handmade soaps using only all natural ingredients. There are no artificial fragrances, colorants, or chemicals. Just the good stuff.

 

What's Inside.

All of our soaps begin with a nourishing base of ingredients that have been carefully chosen to promote clean and healthy skin.

Olive Oil - Rich in vitamin E. Helps to heal dry, itchy skin. Aides in relieving symptoms of psoriasis and eczema.

Almond Oil - Improves skin absorption, removes impurities and dead skin cells. Rich in vitamins A,B & E.

Coconut Oil - Known for anti-aging and moisture retaining benefits. Also has antimicrobial and disinfectant properties.

Castor Oil - Aides in production of collogen and elastin while also deep cleaning pores.

Shea Butter - Ability to enhance collogen production in the skin. Rich in Vitamins A&E.

Beeswax - Seals in moisture without clogging pores. Has natural antibacterial properties for healthy skin.

Cocoa Butter - Known to soothe skin, fight signs of aging & improve skin tone. Helps create a thick, creamy lather.
